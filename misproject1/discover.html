Discover University of Minnesota Duluth

Why I chose UMD and what makes Duluth a great place to learn and live.

Why I chose UMD

I chose the University of Minnesota Duluth because of its strong programs, supportive faculty, and the hands-on opportunities available campus wide. UMD's campus community allows for close and collaborative projects that can help me grow academically and professionally.

What I like about Duluth

Duluth offers a unique combination of natural beauty and active community life. The access to Lake Superior, hiking trails, rivers and creeks, and a vibrant local scene makes it easy to balance academics with the ability to explore the outdoors.
